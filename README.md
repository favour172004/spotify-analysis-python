## 📌 Project Overview

This project explores a Spotify songs dataset using Python, Pandas, and Matplotlib. The goal was to clean the data, perform exploratory data analysis (EDA), and visualize trends in artists, genres, popularity, and song characteristics.

## 🎯 Analysis Questions

This project answers the following questions:

- Who are the most popular artists on sportify?
- Which artists have the most songs on Spotify?
- How has music energy changed over the years?
- What is the most common music genre?

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains Spotify song information, including:

- Artist
- Song Title
- Genre
- Popularity
- Year
- Energy
- Danceability
- Tempo
- Loudness
- Valence
- Acousticness
- Speechiness
- Instrumentalness
- Liveness
- key
- mode

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Jupyter Notebook

## 📊 Key Findings


- Pop is the most common genre in the dataset, with over 1,500 songs.
- A small number of artists contribute a large share of the songs in the dataset.
- Music energy remained fairly consistent (around 0.70–0.78) across most years.
- There was a noticeable dip in average music energy between 2016 and 2019, followed by a sharp increase in 2020.
- The unusually low energy value in 1998 is likely due to the small number of songs available for that year in the dataset.
